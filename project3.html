Michael CO – Talent & Opportunities Platform

I developed Michael CO, a mobile application that showcases African talents and connects them with job opportunities. The platform gives visibility to creatives and professionals while helping recruiters and companies easily discover talent. It blends community, technology, and opportunity into a single ecosystem where talent meets demand.

Goal

Build a scalable platform where users can create profiles, highlight their skills, and access opportunities across industries. The goal was to empower African talent by giving them visibility and connecting them directly with recruiters and employers.

Challenge

The challenge was creating an app that could balance talent discovery with real job opportunities while staying lightweight and accessible. I focused on building a smooth onboarding flow, AI-powered discovery, and reliable performance across devices to make it useful for both talents and recruiters.

Result

Michael CO is now live on the App Store, supporting talents across Africa. The app helps young professionals showcase their skills, apply for jobs, and connect with recruiters. It demonstrates how technology can bridge gaps in visibility and opportunity, making talent discovery easier and more impactful.
