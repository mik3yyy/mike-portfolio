Sales Analysis Dashboard – UI Design

I designed a clean and intuitive sales dashboard that allows businesses to monitor daily revenue, conversion trends, top-selling products, and regional performance. The layout focuses on quick insight delivery and ease of use.

Goal

Create a simple, responsive interface that helps sales teams track performance against goals, visualize trends over time, and identify top-performing segments. The dashboard had to be readable at a glance and adaptable to different screen sizes.

Challenge

Presenting multiple data points without overwhelming users was a core challenge. I used a grid-based layout, clear data hierarchies, and color-coded elements to keep the experience informative yet lightweight.

Result

The final dashboard is fast, user-friendly, and insight-driven—providing actionable sales data at a glance. It supports decision-making across teams and serves as a reliable performance snapshot in both web and tablet formats.
