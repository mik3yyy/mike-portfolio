ABN Realtors – Mobile App Development

I developed the ABN Realtors mobile application to make real estate browsing seamless and reliable. Built with Flutter, the app allows users to explore premium property listings, apply advanced filters, view locations through map integration, and connect directly with verified agents.

Goal

Build a scalable, mobile-first real estate platform where users can browse verified listings, schedule viewings, and communicate securely with agents. The goal was to deliver a powerful property search experience with performance and accessibility in mind.

Challenge

The challenge was ensuring fast rendering of media-rich property listings while keeping the app lightweight. I focused on optimizing API integration, enabling high-performance search and filter tools, and maintaining smooth navigation even with large datasets.

Result

The final application is a responsive, high-performance mobile app that simplifies real estate discovery. Users can now filter, view, and connect with agents in real time, making property browsing faster, smarter, and more convenient—all from their phones.
