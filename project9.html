Football Live Scores App – Mobile UI/UX Design

I designed a fast, responsive football app that delivers real-time match scores, team stats, and fixtures to fans. The interface is built to prioritize speed, readability, and live interaction—keeping users updated during every moment of the game.

Goal

Build a mobile-first platform that allows users to track live scores, follow their favorite teams, and get instant notifications. The UI needed to be clear under pressure and easy to navigate during fast-paced matches.

Challenge

Delivering real-time updates without overwhelming users was a key challenge. I focused on clean typography, modular components, and intuitive layouts that display the most relevant info at a glance—especially on small screens.

Result

The final product is a sleek, high-performance app that delivers live football scores with minimal lag and maximum usability. Fans can follow matches in real-time and stay connected to the game wherever they are.
