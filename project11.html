Reno – Home Renovation Landing Page Design

I designed the landing page for Reno, a home renovation company, to communicate quality, professionalism, and trust. The page features clean visuals, service highlights, and strong calls-to-action to drive inquiries and showcase past work.

Goal

Create a modern, user-friendly landing page that clearly presents Reno’s renovation services and encourages visitors to book consultations. The design needed to feel both aspirational and accessible.

Challenge

The challenge was balancing high-end visual aesthetics with fast load times and mobile responsiveness. I used subtle animations, modular layouts, and trust-building content to guide users smoothly through the page.

Result

The final design is a clean, responsive landing page that highlights Reno’s services and craftsmanship while converting visitors through a clear, intuitive flow. It’s optimized for both desktop and mobile, with a focus on user trust and action.
