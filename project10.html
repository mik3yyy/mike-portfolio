Carland – Car Rental Mobile App Design

I designed the Carland Rental App to streamline how users browse, book, and manage car rentals. The design emphasizes ease of use, with a responsive layout, location-based listings, and transparent pricing to help users make quick, informed decisions.

Goal

Create a user-first car rental app that offers fast browsing, flexible booking options, and easy access to rental history. The experience needed to be sleek, trustworthy, and optimized for mobile users on the move.

Challenge

Ensuring clarity around availability, pricing, and rental terms was critical. I focused on clear visual hierarchy, smooth filters, and real-time location support to simplify the decision-making process without sacrificing detail.

Result

The final app delivers a seamless car rental experience—complete with user profiles, booking management, and secure payment integration. Carland now offers users a modern, trusted way to rent vehicles on the go.
